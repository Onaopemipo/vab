About Lola

LOLA (Lagos On-line Assistance) Lola was created for Lagos state property to enumerate property owners details Lola’s land use charge payment is been automated to receive payments periodically from registered users. As property owner provide their details and consent, LOLA implement payment as at when due, implementing appropriate deductions and increments when required.

Benefits of LOLA

  • Improved Customer Service
  • Monitoring Tax Payers data and gaining insights
  • Added Convenience in Tax Collection ( easier and smoother way for tax payers)
  • Easily accessible to international Tax Payers
  • Multiple Channels (WhatsApp, Facebook, Telegram & Skype)
  • Autonomous Reasoning (chatbots can take decisions based on pre-programmed rules)
  • 24 hours availability
  • Aggregated Data for better reporting

Why LOLA?

  • Secured & Encrypted Communication
  • Data Validation Checks (the proposed chatbot can detect errors and request that the tax payer corrects it)
  • Added Convenience in Tax Collection (easier and smoother way for tax payers)
  • Multiple Payment Integrations
  • Instant QR Code Generation & Verification

  • Marketing Funnel Guidance (chatbots won't keep tax payers waiting. It can attend to all users at the same time)
  • Admin Portal for Analysis and Reporting
  • Send Bills/Receipt through Social Media Channels
  • Feedback Analysis and Learning
